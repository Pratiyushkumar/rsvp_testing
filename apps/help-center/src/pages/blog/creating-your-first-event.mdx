# Creating Your First Event: A Step-by-Step Guide

Welcome to our RSVP Event SaaS platform! This guide will walk you through the process of creating your first event step by step.

## Step 1: Log In to Your Account

1. Go to the **login page** and sign in using your credentials.
2. If you don’t have an account yet, click **Sign Up** and follow the registration process.

## Step 2: Access the Event Creation Page

1. After logging in, navigate to the **Dashboard**.
2. Click on the **Create Event** button, usually located in the top-right corner.

## Step 3: Fill in Basic Event Details

1. Provide the essential details for your event:
   - **Event Title**: A clear and descriptive name for your event.
   - **Date & Time**: Select the date and start time for your event.
   - **Location**: Choose whether the event is physical or virtual.
     - For physical events, enter the venue address.
     - For virtual events, add a meeting link (e.g., Zoom, Google Meet).

## Step 4: Add an Event Description

1. Write a compelling event description to attract attendees.
   - Include key details such as the agenda, guest speakers, and any prerequisites for attendees.
   - Use formatting tools (if available) to make your text visually appealing.

## Step 5: Customize RSVP Settings

1. Choose your RSVP options:

   - Enable/disable RSVP limits (e.g., cap attendance at 100 people).
   - Add a deadline for RSVPs to close.
   - Customize the RSVP form to collect attendee information like name, email, and dietary preferences.

2. You can also set up:
   - **Automated reminders** for attendees.
   - **Confirmation messages** or emails.

## Step 6: Upload Media and Branding (Optional)

1. Add an event banner or logo to make your event page visually appealing.
2. Upload additional resources like PDFs, images, or videos, if necessary.

## Step 7: Review and Publish

1. Double-check all the event details to ensure accuracy.
2. Click the **Preview** button to see how your event page will appear to attendees.
3. Once satisfied, hit **Publish** to make your event live.

## Step 8: Share Your Event

1. After publishing, you’ll receive a unique event URL.
2. Share this link via email, social media, or other channels to invite attendees.

## Step 9: Manage Your Event

1. Go to the **My Events** section to view and manage your events.
2. Monitor RSVPs in real time, send updates to attendees, or make changes to event details if needed.

Congratulations, your event is now live! With our platform’s user-friendly tools, managing events and RSVPs has never been easier. If you encounter any issues or need further assistance, check out our [Help Center](/) or contact support.

Happy event planning!
